Project: Mix-Use Building for Older Adults

Location: Ponce, PR

Profesor: Luis V Badillo

Profesor: Maria Julia Escalona

School Work: PUCPR School of Architecture

Designer: Luis A Bravo Santiago

This building is located in the town of Ponce Puerto Rico . This building is made for senior residents. It consists of a business office, commercial premises for rent , restaurant for residents as well for the public, administration office, green roofs, solar panels , parking for residents, pickup place for ambulance and much more . This building was designed entirely by myself.
