This is a master plan project for Castañer located in Lares, Puerto Rico. This project was made to renovate the entire sector. We changed roads, created new facilities, even made a hotel for tourist. Due to how many seniors live here we created an seniors home where they can live and be surrounded by people of there same age. Also we wanted to recreate the growing of vegetables for them to sell in their Farmers Market. We are offering that Farmers Market for residents that live inside this sector. This Project was created by a group of 5 students including myself and by far is one of the biggest project I’ve been involved so far.
